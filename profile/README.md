**Jitsi Meet** is a leading open-source platform for secure, high-quality video conferences. Unlike many commercial solutions, **Jitsi Meeting** requires no accounts, subscriptions, or time limits, making it the ideal choice for instant collaboration. It runs directly in your browser, offering a seamless experience for teams, educators, and friends.

With **Jitsi Meet**, users can enjoy:
- Crystal-clear audio and video calls with no participant cap.
- End-to-end encryption for maximum privacy and security.
- Powerful features like screen sharing, integrated chat, and live streaming.
- Easy integration and self-hosting capabilities for full control.

The platform is trusted worldwide for its reliability and is perfect for scenarios like **Jitsi Meeting** for remote work, **JitsiMeet** for online classrooms, or quick catch-ups. Its commitment to open-source ensures continuous innovation and transparency.
